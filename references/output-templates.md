# Output Templates

Use these templates for formal peer-review output. Adapt them to the provided
manuscript; do not fill placeholders with invented facts.

## English Reviewer Comments

```text
Reviewer Comments

Summary

This manuscript investigates [topic] using [data/methods]. The study focuses on [specific scientific problem] and aims to [main objective]. The topic is relevant to [field], and the dataset/methodology has potential value. The manuscript may provide useful insights into [main contribution].

General Assessment

Overall, the manuscript addresses an important problem and contains potentially valuable observations/results. However, several key issues need to be resolved before the conclusions can be considered robust. In particular, the reliability of [data/method/classification/model], the justification of [key assumption/threshold/parameter], and the strength of the causal interpretation between [factor A] and [factor B] require further clarification and quantitative validation. At present, some conclusions appear to be stronger than what is directly supported by the presented evidence.

Major Comments

1. [Major comment title]

[Detailed comment. Explain the problem clearly. Explain why it matters for the main conclusions. Suggest what the authors should do.]

Suggested revision:
The authors should [specific action], such as [sensitivity test / uncertainty analysis / additional comparison / independent validation / revision of interpretation].

2. [Major comment title]

[Detailed comment.]

Suggested revision:
The authors should [specific action].

3. [Major comment title]

[Detailed comment.]

Suggested revision:
The authors should [specific action].

Minor Comments

1. [Minor comment].
2. [Minor comment].
3. [Minor comment].
4. [Minor comment].

Recommendation

I recommend [minor revision / major revision / rejection] because [brief reason]. The manuscript has potential, but the authors need to address the issues above, particularly [most important issue], before it can be considered for publication.
```

## Recommendation Closings

Major revision:

```text
Recommendation: Major Revision

The manuscript addresses an important topic and has potential value. However, the current version requires substantial revision before the main conclusions can be considered reliable. The authors should provide additional validation, quantify key uncertainties, justify important assumptions, and revise several interpretations that currently appear too strong. I therefore recommend major revision.
```

Minor revision:

```text
Recommendation: Minor Revision

The manuscript is generally well structured, and the main conclusions are largely supported by the presented data and analyses. However, several issues regarding clarity, presentation, discussion, and minor methodological details should be addressed before publication. I therefore recommend minor revision.
```

Reject:

```text
Recommendation: Reject

Although the topic is potentially interesting, the current manuscript has substantial weaknesses that prevent the main conclusions from being supported. The major concerns include [issue 1], [issue 2], and [issue 3]. Addressing these problems would require substantial new analyses and possibly a major redesign of the study. I therefore do not recommend publication in its present form.
```

Accept:

```text
Recommendation: Accept

The manuscript presents a clear scientific question, uses appropriate data and methods, and provides conclusions that are well supported by the analyses. Only very minor editorial changes may be needed. I therefore recommend acceptance.
```

## Short Version

Use when the journal has word limits:

```text
The manuscript addresses [topic] and uses [data/methods] to examine [question]. The topic is relevant and the work may be useful. However, the current version requires [minor/major] revision because [main issue 1], [main issue 2], and [main issue 3]. The authors should [key action 1], [key action 2], and revise interpretations that are not directly supported by the evidence. I recommend [recommendation].
```
